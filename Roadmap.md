# 🚀 40-Day English Fluency Roadmap for Developers
> **Focus: Professional Communication, Technical Vocabulary, and Confidence.**

---

## ⚙️ The Daily Sprint Formula (60 Minutes)
* **00–10 min:** Anki/Flashcard review (Technical & Common terms).
* **10–40 min:** Focused Daily Task (listed below).
* **40–60 min:** **The Developer's Monologue:** Explain a piece of code you wrote today or a technical concept (e.g., "How does a `map` function work?") out loud. Record and listen for "umms."

## 🟨 Week 2: Survival Grammar for Devs
*Goal: Stop mental translation; start using correct sentence structures.*

| Day | Topic | Practice Source | The "How-To" Practice |
|:--- |:--- |:--- |:--- |
| **8** | Present Simple | [English with Lucy](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4IY2bTSEXUQ) | **Daily Standup:** Write 5 sentences about your daily habits (e.g., "I check my Jira tickets at 9 AM"). |
| **9** | Past Simple | [BBC Learning](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iHEJhFvWVM8) | **The Log:** Describe what you finished yesterday: "I fixed the bug," "I pushed the code." |
| **10** | Present Perfect | [Perfect English Grammar](https://www.perfect-english-grammar.com/present-perfect.html) | **Status Update:** Practice "I have finished..." vs "I finished." Use "I have just..." for recent tasks. |
| **11** | Future (Will/Going to) | [Will vs Going to](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oL8KMjKRaAg) | **Roadmap:** Use "going to" for planned features and "will" for spontaneous offers. |
| **12** | Conditionals (If) | [Conditionals Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=8v8r65NDGFQ) | **Logic Flow:** Practice "If/Then" logic: "If the API fails, then the app displays an error." |
| **13** | Prepositions | [In/On/At](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=h7Ej2Pk8gKw) | **Environment:** Describe your UI: "The button is **on** the top, **in** the corner." |
| **14** | Review | [12 Tenses Summary](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=d7LD7cUNFkA) | **Tense Switching:** Narrate a project from start (past), current (present), to finish (future). |

| **40** | 🎉 Milestone | No Video | **The English-Only Day:** Think, write, and speak *only* in English for 24 hours. |
